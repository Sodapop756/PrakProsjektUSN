Core Topics

What you need to know

Four key areas every student should understand before using AI in their studies.

📚
Academic Integrity

Understand the difference between ethical AI assistance and academic dishonesty. Learn when and how AI use must be declared.

🛡️
Privacy & Data

Know what information you should never share with AI tools, and how your data may be stored or used by AI providers.

🔍
Hallucinations & Bias

AI can produce confident-sounding misinformation. Learn how to fact-check AI output and recognise bias in AI-generated content.

🎯
Ethical Prompting

Ask questions that help you learn — not ones that do the learning for you. Get more from AI while staying academically honest.
